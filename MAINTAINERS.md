## Maintainer recruitment

openZro is **actively seeking additional maintainers** to broaden the
contributor base ahead of foundation-hosting eligibility. Particularly
welcome:

- Contributors with deep WireGuard or Go-networking experience for
  the `client/`, `iface/` and `relay/` areas.
- Frontend engineers with Next.js 14 / React 18 / Tailwind for
  `dashboard/`.
- Operators with Kubernetes / Helm / GitOps experience for
  [openzro/helms](https://github.com/openzro/helms) and
  [openzro/openzro-operator](https://github.com/openzro/openzro-operator).
- Anyone willing to triage issues, review PRs and shepherd RFCs.

If you've been contributing and want to step up, open an issue with
the `maintainer-candidate` label or reach out to the lead maintainer.

## Security-issue contact

For coordinated disclosure of vulnerabilities, see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
Do **not** open public issues for security reports — use the private
advisory flow.
